What makes Shifa University Unique?

Shifa Tameer-e-Millat University, a private educational institution, was established in 2012 and is situated in Islamabad, a major city in Pakistan with a population of between 1 and 5 million people. Shifa Tameer-e-Millat Université is a coeducational institution of higher learning that has received official accreditation or recognition from the Pakistan Higher Education Commission. Pakistan. Shifa Tameer-e-Millat University provides courses and programmes leading to bachelor degrees in a number of fields of study, as well as other higher education degrees that are legally recognised. Additionally, Shifa Tameer-e-Millat University offers students a variety of academic and non-academic resources and services, including as a library and administrative services.

Documents Required

Prepare all necessary papers before applying for admission to Shifa University in Pakistan.

  • Currently available photograph (1 inch square, white background): (Four Copies)
  • Character Certificate: (One Copy)
  • Matric / SSC / O – Level : (Two Copies)
  • Intermediate / HSSC / A – Level : (Two Copies)
  • Student CNIC/B Form: (Two Copies)
  • Domicile: (Two Copies)
  • CNIC of the parent or legal guardian: (Two Copies)
  • The result of the admission test for the MCAT, ETEA, NTS, ETS, or HEC, as applies: Original
  • NOC for other Board/University/Project: Original Stamp Paper submitted after the admission
  • A certificate of mental and physical fitness must be submitted: after the admittance Original

    • Your documents will be rejected by the institution whether they are presented online or offline. Make sure that all of the necessary documents and images are attested through your school, college, or by a teacher of the 17th grade or higher.
