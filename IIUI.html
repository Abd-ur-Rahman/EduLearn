Documents Required

Prepare all necessary papers before applying for admission to Air University in Pakistan.

  • Currently available photograph (1 inch square, white background): (Four Copies)
  • Character Certificate: (One Copy)
  • Matric / SSC / O – Level : (Two Copies)
  • Intermediate / HSSC / A – Level : (Two Copies)
  • Student CNIC/B Form: (Two Copies)
  • Domicile: (Two Copies)
  • CNIC of the parent or legal guardian: (Two Copies)
  • The result of the admission test for the MCAT, ETEA, NTS, ETS, or HEC, as applies: Original
  • NOC for other Board/University/Project: Original Stamp Paper submitted after the admission
  • A certificate of mental and physical fitness must be submitted: after the admittance Original

    • Your documents will be rejected by the institution whether they are presented online or offline. Make sure that all of the necessary documents and images are attested through your school, college, or by a teacher of the 17th grade or higher.

UNIVERSITY TOUR

An Islamic university that offers both secular and religious education is IIUI. Although I'm not familiar with the male sector, I can speak to the advantages for girls because I'm in it. Girls and boys being separated and given the freedom to engage in all activities is, in my opinion, a really beneficial development. I was too nervous to play any games while I was in co-ed, but now that I'm here, this institution has given me a platform that allows me to play any game without fear, and I'm now on the basketball team.

Additionally, since this uni is an international organisation with students from various nations, you have the opportunity to interact with people from both home and abroad, learn more about what students from other nations think of your home country, and have a better forum for exchanging opinions.
