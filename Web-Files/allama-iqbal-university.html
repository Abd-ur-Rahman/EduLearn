Allama Iqbal Open University

What makes Allama Iqbal University Unique?

It was Asia's first open university and was founded in 1974. It places a great focus on delivering online education in the fields of engineering, law, philosophy, and natural and social science. It is recognised as one of the most alluring universities in the nation and receives the most applications from prospective students each year. In a wide range of academic fields, the institution provides broad undergraduate, graduate, and doctorate study programmes. The institution was founded as a public university in 1974 after seeing the success of Open University in the United Kingdom.

How to apply in this University

You must confirm your eligibility requirements for the particular programme or degree you intend to apply for before applying to this university by visiting this link. Eligibility Criteria

  • Click on this link to register for Air University. Admission Portal
  • Fill up the required information
  • Choose a Program or Degree
  • Check Eligibility Criteria for this Program or Degree
  • Apply for the Program or Degree (Online / Offline)
  • Download Chalan Form or Submit it in Required Bank
  • Complete Admission Tasks
  • Got Selected
  • Go for Study

Documents Required

Prepare all necessary papers before applying for admission to Air University in Pakistan.

  • Currently available photograph (1 inch square, white background): (Four Copies)
  • Character Certificate: (One Copy)
  • Matric / SSC / O – Level : (Two Copies)
  • Intermediate / HSSC / A – Level : (Two Copies)
  • Student CNIC/B Form: (Two Copies)
  • Domicile: (Two Copies)
  • CNIC of the parent or legal guardian: (Two Copies)
  • The result of the admission test for the MCAT, ETEA, NTS, ETS, or HEC, as applies: Original
  • NOC for other Board/University/Project: Original Stamp Paper submitted after the admission
  • A certificate of mental and physical fitness must be submitted: after the admittance Original

    • Your documents will be rejected by the institution whether they are presented online or offline. Make sure that all of the necessary documents and images are attested through your school, college, or by a teacher of the 17th grade or higher.
