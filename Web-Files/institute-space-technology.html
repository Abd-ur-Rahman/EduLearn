Institute of Space Technology Islamabad

What makes Institute of Space Technology Unique?

Both a graduate programme in Communication Engineering and an undergraduate programme in Materials Science & Engineering were introduced by the institute in Fall 2008. Instead of offering a Communication Systems Engineering undergraduate degree, the school will begin offering an extra Electrical Engineering undergraduate programme in the autumn of 2011. In addition to these native programmes, the institution has partnerships with Beihang University (BUAA), Northern Polytechnic University (NPU), China, and University of Surrey (UniS), UK, to provide connected master's degrees in over a dozen different fields. Higher Education Commission and Pakistan Engineering Council approval and accreditation are given to IST's undergraduate programmes.
