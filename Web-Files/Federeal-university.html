What makes Federal Urdu University Islamabad Unique?

The Federal Urdu Institution of Arts, Science & Technology is proud to hold the distinction of being Pakistan's first university to formally choose Urdu as its primary medium of instruction and instruction. According to the President of Pakistan's Ordinance No. CXIX of 2002, the institution was founded on November 13th, 2002. According to this ordinance's provisions, the university's main location is in Islamabad. By assuming control of two federal government institutions, the Federal Urdu Science College and Federal Urdu Arts College, the university was able to begin operations in November 2002.

The principal seat of the university at Islamabad commenced its teaching programs in November 2003. To begin with, the university established two faculties viz Faculty of Economics and Management Sciences and Faculty of Information Technology and Computer Science with immediate focus on teaching the subjects of Business Administration and Computer Science. The university may establish any number of colleges, institutes and campuses anywhere in or out side Pakistan as may be prescribed.
