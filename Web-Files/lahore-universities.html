WELCOME TO LAHORE

About City

With a population of more than 13 million, Lahore is the second-most populous city in Pakistan after Karachi. One of Pakistan's most socially liberal, forward-thinking, and cosmopolitan cities, it is both the country's largest metropolis and the historic cultural hub of the greater Punjab area.

Education

With more colleges and institutions than any other city in Pakistan, Lahore is referred to be the country's educational hub. 74% of Lahore's population is literate. The city has Pakistan's sole future hyper high-tech hub and produces the majority of the country's experts in the disciplines of science, technology, IT, law, engineering, medical, nuclear sciences, pharmacology, telecommunication, biotechnology, microelectronics, and nanotechnology.

Tourism

In Pakistan, Lahore is still a popular tourist destination. UNESCO World Heritage Sites may be found in the Walled City of Lahore, which underwent renovation in 2014. The Lahore Fort, which is close to the Walled City and has the Sheesh Mahal, the Alamgiri Gate, the Naulakha pavilion, and the Moti Masjid, is among the most well-known sites. Since 1981, the fort and the nearby Shalimar Gardens have been a UNESCO World Heritage Site.
