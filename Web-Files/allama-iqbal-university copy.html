What makes Air University Unique?

The institution was founded in 2002 by the Pakistan Air Force (PAF). It has been granted civilian status and provides undergraduate, graduate, and doctorate study programmes. The PAF's education command oversees the functioning of the university. Humanities, business management, and engineering are prioritised at the university.

According to the HEC, the institution is among the top 10 universities in the nation for engineering and technology.

The Pakistan Engineering Council and the Higher Education Commission of Pakistan are partners with Air University. The college belongs to the Association of Commonwealth Universities of the UK.

Documents Required

Prepare all necessary papers before applying for admission to Allama Iqbal Open University in Pakistan.

  • Currently available photograph (1 inch square, white background): (Four Copies)
  • Current photo (passport size, white background): (Four Copies)
  • Character Certificate: (One Copy)
  • Matric / SSC / O – Level : (Two Copies)
  • Intermediate / HSSC / A – Level : (Two Copies)
  • Student CNIC/B Form: (Two Copies)
  • Domicile: (Two Copies)
  • CNIC of the parent or legal guardian: (Two Copies)
  • The result of the admission test for the MCAT, ETEA, NTS, ETS, or HEC, as applies: Original
  • NOC for other Board/University/Project: Original Stamp Paper submitted after the admission
  • A certificate of mental and physical fitness must be submitted: after the admittance Original

Your documents will be rejected by the institution whether they are presented online or offline. Make sure that all of the necessary documents and images are attested through your school, college, or by a teacher of the 17th grade or higher.
