National SKills University Islamabad

What makes National SKills University Unique?

The Federal Pakistani government founded the non-profit, public National Skills University in Islamabad.

The first public institution founded on the grounds of the rapid employment transformation issues brought on by the Fourth Industrial Revolution is National Skills University Islamabad (IR 4.0). This higher education institution is different from other higher education institutions in that it places more emphasis on teaching students skills that will prepare them for the workforce than it does on their cognitive growth.

Due to this university's educational procedures emphasising more on practical experience relevant to a subject, students are expected to also have extensive understanding of industries in their area of expertise. This will entail both laboratory training at the university and fieldwork with this institution's industrial partners through Co-op programmes.

Documents Required

Prepare all necessary papers before applying for admission to Air University in Pakistan.

  • Currently available photograph (1 inch square, white background): (Four Copies)
  • Character Certificate: (One Copy)
  • Matric / SSC / O – Level : (Two Copies)
  • Intermediate / HSSC / A – Level : (Two Copies)
  • Student CNIC/B Form: (Two Copies)
  • Domicile: (Two Copies)
  • CNIC of the parent or legal guardian: (Two Copies)
  • The result of the admission test for the MCAT, ETEA, NTS, ETS, or HEC, as applies: Original
  • NOC for other Board/University/Project: Original Stamp Paper submitted after the admission
  • A certificate of mental and physical fitness must be submitted: after the admittance Original

One of the top institutions in the nation is Air University in Islamabad. Comparing to a number of the older universities in the nation, it is relatively young.
