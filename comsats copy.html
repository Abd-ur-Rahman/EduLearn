COMSATS University Islamabad (CUI)

What makes COMSATS Unique?

International organisation known as COMSATS stands for the Commission on Science and Technology for Sustainable Development in the South. It seeks to bridge the rising technological and scientific divide between the developed and developing worlds. Under the direction of Nobel Laureate Dr. Abdus Salam, the Third World Academy of Sciences (TWAS) proposed the creation of COMSATS.

The COMSATS founding meeting took place in Islamabad on October 4 and 5, 1994. Attendees came from 36 different nations. Twenty-two ministers, members of Islamabad's diplomatic corps, and officials of international organisations including UNESCO, UNIDO, UNEP, and the World Bank were among the attendees.

Documents Required

Prepare all necessary papers before applying for admission in Capital University in Pakistan.

  • Currently available photograph (1 inch square, white background): (Four Copies)
  • Current photo (passport size, white background): (Four Copies)
  • Character Certificate: (One Copy)
  • Matric / SSC / O – Level : (Two Copies)
  • Intermediate / HSSC / A – Level : (Two Copies)
  • Student CNIC/B Form: (Two Copies)
  • Domicile: (Two Copies)
  • CNIC of the parent or legal guardian: (Two Copies)
  • The result of the admission test for the MCAT, ETEA, NTS, ETS, or HEC, as applies: Original
  • NOC for other Board/University/Project: Original Stamp Paper submitted after the admission
  • A certificate of mental and physical fitness must be submitted: after the admittance Original

    • If not, your documents will be rejected by the institution whether they are presented online or offline. Make sure that all of the necessary documents and images are attested through your school, college, or by a teacher of the 17th grade or higher.
