What makes Muslim Youth University Unique?

MY University serves as a platform for education. With a strong participatory culture, it is dedicated to providing high-quality instruction. We have thus developed a robust system of assistance for both students and staff. Professionalism, teamwork, respect, honesty, mentoring, systems thinking, and communication skills are all things they focus on. As it is our aim to see the school at the pinnacle of quality in the days to come, we plan to recruit a broad intake of students - the finest and truest of a kind.

Documents Required

Prepare all necessary papers before applying for admission to Air University in Pakistan.

  • Currently available photograph (1 inch square, white background): (Four Copies)
  • Character Certificate: (One Copy)
  • Matric / SSC / O – Level : (Two Copies)
  • Intermediate / HSSC / A – Level : (Two Copies)
  • Student CNIC/B Form: (Two Copies)
  • Domicile: (Two Copies)
  • CNIC of the parent or legal guardian: (Two Copies)
  • The result of the admission test for the MCAT, ETEA, NTS, ETS, or HEC, as applies: Original
  • NOC for other Board/University/Project: Original Stamp Paper submitted after the admission
  • A certificate of mental and physical fitness must be submitted: after the admittance Original

    • Your documents will be rejected by the institution whether they are presented online or offline. Make sure that all of the necessary documents and images are attested through your school, college, or by a teacher of the 17th grade or higher.

UNIVERSITY TOUR

Instead of solely encouraging grade-driven rote learning, our programmes push students' intellectual capabilities to address complicated challenges with a social focus in the actual world. Instead than concentrating solely on exam-based learning, we emphasise project-based learning and problem solving. Our instruction is student-centered rather than teacher-centered, and the programme outcomes are precisely defined and aligned with the learning objectives of the courses.

The only way MY University is able to recruit good professors is by providing a new career, explicitly stated career benchmarks with tenured promotions, and retirement benefits. This is in contrast to the majority of institutions in Pakistan where income is the primary motivating element for teachers. Everyone may discover their enthusiasm and then create a lifelong goal thanks to the supporting atmosphere that MY University offers.
